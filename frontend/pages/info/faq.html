Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about flood safety and using this system.

How do I report an emergency?

You can report an emergency by clicking on the "Report Emergency" button in the navigation menu. Fill out the form with your location and details of the situation. If it is a life-threatening emergency, please call 1122 immediately.

How can I find the nearest shelter?

Go to the "Shelters" page and use the map to locate shelters near you. You can also click the "Use My Location" button to automatically find the closest safe locations.

Are the flood forecasts accurate?

Our forecasts are based on data from the Meteorological Department and real-time river monitoring stations. While we strive for high accuracy, weather conditions can change rapidly. Always stay updated with the latest alerts.

How do I subscribe to alerts?

Currently, alerts are broadcasted on the website. We are working on an SMS and email subscription service which will be available soon.
